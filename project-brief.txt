RunPlayBack Website Rebuild

Goal:
Rebuild runplayback.com as a custom website hosted on Vercel, using Supabase for article drafts, YouTube video imports, affiliate links, and publishing workflow.

Main pages:
- Home
- Partner
- Articles
- Popular Videos
- Contact

Main feature:
Turn each RunPlayBack YouTube video into a written SEO article using the video captions, YouTube description, affiliate links, and RPB Writing Style.

Workflow:
1. Import YouTube video.
2. Pull title, description, thumbnail, captions, and links.
3. Generate article draft.
4. Save article as draft.
5. Review and edit article.
6. Publish article to the website.

Website Navigation:
- Home
- Partner
- Articles
- Popular Videos
- Contact

Home Page:
Introduce RunPlayBack as an EV lifestyle channel focused on electric bikes, scooters, mini bikes, batteries, accessories, and real-world tech tips.

Partner Page:
Explain how brands can collaborate with RunPlayBack for product reviews, sponsored videos, affiliate partnerships, and usage rights.

Articles Page:
A searchable library of written articles created from RunPlayBack YouTube videos.

Popular Videos Page:
Show selected popular RunPlayBack videos with thumbnails, titles, short descriptions, and embedded YouTube links.

Contact Page:
Simple contact form for brand partnerships, viewer questions, and media inquiries.

Article Workflow:
- Admin imports a YouTube video.
- App pulls video title, thumbnail, publish date, description, captions, and video URL.
- App extracts affiliate links and hyperlinks from the YouTube description.
- App creates an article draft in the RPB Writing Style.
- Draft is saved in Supabase.
- Admin can review, edit, and fix typos before publishing.
- Published articles appear on the Articles page.
- Each article embeds the original YouTube video.
- Each article includes links from the original YouTube description.

Architecture Map

GoDaddy:
- Keeps ownership of runplayback.com
- Domain will point to Vercel when the new site is ready

Vercel:
- Hosts the new RunPlayBack website
- Runs the Next.js app
- Handles public website pages and admin dashboard

Supabase:
- Stores article drafts
- Stores published articles
- Stores imported YouTube video data
- Stores affiliate links
- Handles admin login

YouTube API:
- Pulls video title
- Pulls video description
- Pulls thumbnail
- Pulls captions when available
- Pulls video URL

AI Article Generator:
- Turns YouTube captions into RPB-style articles
- Keeps the original meaning of the video
- Formats the article for Google SEO
- Saves the draft for review before publishing

Codex:
- Builds and edits the codebase
- Helps create the Next.js app
- Helps connect Vercel, Supabase, and YouTube

RunPlayBack Article Generator Rules

Goal:
Convert YouTube video captions into a readable article that sounds like RunPlayBack.

Writing Style:
- Friendly
- Casual
- Educational
- No corporate jargon
- No hype language
- No clickbait
- Honest observations
- Real-world riding experience
- Explain technical information in simple terms

Article Structure:

1. Introduction
- Explain what product is being reviewed
- Explain who it is for

2. First Impressions
- Initial thoughts
- Design
- Build quality

3. Technical Specifications
- Battery
- Motor
- Controller
- Suspension
- Brakes
- Tires
- Weight
- Range

4. Real World Experience
- Ride quality
- Comfort
- Performance
- Handling

5. Pros
- Bullet list

6. Cons
- Bullet list

7. Final Thoughts
- Summary
- Who should buy it
- Who should skip it

8. Video
- Embedded YouTube video

9. Links
- Affiliate links
- Manufacturer links
